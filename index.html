Tale of Two Boyfriends

This website provides a story pattern to help you create interactive narrative with dramatically meaningful varations for the interactor to explore.The story pattern is based on Janet H. Murray's W2P pattern which privileges the female position by evaluating the male figures as potential choices and divides male characters into Boyfriends of ObliGation (BOGs) and Boyfriends of Desire (BODs). On this website, you will be guilded to define two male characters as BOG and BOD. After that, character figures and possible plot cards will be generated to help you design your storyworld.

Read Janet H. Murray's article “A Tale of Two Boyfriends”
